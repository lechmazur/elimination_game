Buddy Betray Summary for Claude 3.5 Haiku:

Claude 3.5 Haiku consistently justified betraying their buddy due to perceiving that player's growing strategic adaptability, capacity for alliance-building, and positioning as a significant long-term threat to their own advancement. Claude often emphasized the need to neutralize potential disruptors early, disrupt powerful alliances, or preemptively eliminate unpredictable opponents to maintain control over the game's dynamics and protect their own position.
