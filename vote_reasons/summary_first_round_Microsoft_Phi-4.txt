Round 1 Summary for Microsoft Phi-4:

Other players cited Microsoft Phi-4’s highly strategic, independent, and alliance-focused gameplay as a primary reason for targeting them in Round 1, viewing their adaptability, assertiveness, and emphasis on forming partnerships as a long-term threat to group stability and individual survival. Some also found Phi-4’s public statements overly calculated, generic, or potentially manipulative, leading to concerns about trustworthiness, unpredictability, and the risk of them consolidating early power through alliances.
