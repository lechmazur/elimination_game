Jury Elimination Summary for Qwen 2.5 Max:

Qwen 2.5 Max was eliminated because the jury viewed its gameplay as lacking consistent transparency and reliability, often prioritizing opportunistic shifts and self-preservation over collaborative trust and fairness. While Qwen 2.5 Max demonstrated adaptability and some strategic acumen, jurors cited concerns about alliance-flipping, unpredictability, and actions that sometimes undermined stable coalition-building compared to the more principled and consistently collaborative approaches of its opponents.
